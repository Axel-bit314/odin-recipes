Sopes

"Sopes Comida Mexicana" by Distrito Medico Monterrey is lincensed under CC BY 2.0

Description

This recipe is extremely easy to make for the family and can be a wondering food to make if your planning to change things up. Sopes are a well known food in Mexian culture and it is enjoyed by millions around the world. Sopes in many Mexican household are home made and while there are many places where you can buy sopes at your convinence there is nothing better then making and eating you own sopes with the family.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups masa harina
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 cups warm water, or as needed
  • 1 quart oil for frying

Steps to Follow:

  1. Whisk masa harina and salt together in a bowl. Stir in enough water until dough is smooth and holds together.
  2. Form dough into tweleve 2-inch balls. Place balls onto parchment paper and flatten to 1/2-inch thick circles.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 400 degrees F (200 degress C)
