Mexican Sopes that are easy to make

This recipe is extremely easy to make for the family and can be a wondering food to make if your planning to change things up. Sopes are a well known food in Mexian culture and it is enjoyed by millions around the world. Sopes in many Mexican household are home made and while there are many places where you can buy sopes at your convinence there is nothing better then making and eating you own sopes with the family.
